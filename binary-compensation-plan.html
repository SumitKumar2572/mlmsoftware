How Does Binary MLM Plan Works?

In the Binary Plan, the recruitment process begins with each member at level one bringing in two sponsors or distributors. These recruits form the foundation of the plan and are split into two legs: the right leg and the left leg. As the network grows, each leg branches out further, creating additional networks or legs

How is the Commission Calculated?

The leg with more members is called the Power Leg, and the leg with fewer distributors is the Weaker Leg.

In the Binary MLM software compensation plan, the commission is determined based on the points earned by the Weaker Leg. Here, the compensation is not calculated based on the number of levels but as per the total points earned.

Therefore, if the Weaker Leg earns 1000 points and the Power Leg earns 2000 points, the commission will be 10% of 1000 points, which shall be distributed among all the sponsors equally.
